About Us

We are NOORUL HUDA ASSOCIATION the official Students’ Union, a body of passionate and dedicated representatives elected by and for the student community. Our purpose is to propogate islam and preparing students for it, achieve college's aims and goals, organize meaningful activities, and provide platforms for leadership, creativity, and collaboration. We believe that college life is not just about academics, but also about building lifelong skills, friendships, and memories. Through spiritual practices, cultural events, academic support and social initiatives, we aim to create an environment where every student feels valued, supported, and inspired to achieve their fullest potential.

visibility Vision

“To be a vibrant and inclusive student community where every soul is moulded spiritually , every talent is nurtured, and every student is empowered to shape a better tomorrow.”

flag Mission

  • To reinforce Islamic propagation and to achieve college's aims and goals.
  • To create opportunities for leadership, creativity, and personal growth.
  • To foster unity, inclusivity, and cultural exchange across campus.
  • To promote academic excellence, innovation, and social responsibility.
  • To support student welfare activities.
  • To serve as a bridge between students, faculty, and administration.
